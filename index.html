Our Story

Born from a Love of Exceptional Coffee

Velo Coffee began as a small corner shop in Kemang with a simple mission: serve extraordinary coffee in a space where people actually want to linger. What started as a passion project between two friends has grown into Jakarta's favorite neighborhood coffee destination.

We partner directly with farmers across Colombia, Ethiopia, Guatemala, and Indonesia — ensuring every bean meets our uncompromising standards for quality and ethical sourcing.
